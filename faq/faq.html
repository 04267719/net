FalconNet - Frequently Asked Questions

Is FalconNet 100% free?

Of course! FalconNet provides many high quality movies for free. To earn access to more features such as commenting, reviewing and making profiles, users must pay for one of our monthly memberships. However, paying members receive the same amount of movies in the same quality as free members.

Why are some movies not available yet?

Some movies are not available yet, and we're working on releasing them as soon as possible. We'll notify you when there are new releases on FalconNet that we think you'll enjoy.
